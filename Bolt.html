Bolt

This is Bolt :)

Bolt is a White Swiss Shepard dog and was adopted by a young girl named Penny.
About 5 years after Bolt's adoption, both Bolt and Penny starred in a hit crime fighting television show titled "Bolt".

Bolt and Penny

During Bolt's acting career, he believed everything that was filmed, was actually real. Bolt is a dog, so you really can't blame him.

Characteristics of Bolt

  1. Bolt is very brave and wasn't afraid despite his separation.
  2. Bolt was determined to get back to Penny.
  3. Bolt was optimistic until the very end of his journey towards rejoining Penny.
  4. Bolt was also very stubborn when he was told his super powers weren't real.

Bolt's Friends :/

  • Penny, Bolt's owner and has raised him since the start of the film.
  • Mittens, a short-tempered, sassy, and pessimistic cat who eventually opens up to Bolt during the film.
  • Rhino, although not the brightest character, Rhino is a crazy fan of Bolt's films and wants to do anything to assist him.
